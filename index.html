About Maa Bricks

Maa Bricks is a leading manufacturer of high-quality red bricks, proudly serving Mangaldoi, Assam, and surrounding regions. With years of experience in the industry, we are committed to providing durable and reliable bricks for all your construction needs.

Our manufacturing process combines traditional techniques with modern technology to ensure each brick meets stringent quality standards. We believe in sustainable practices and responsible sourcing of materials.

Our Mission: To be the most trusted brick supplier in Assam, contributing to strong and beautiful structures that last for generations.
